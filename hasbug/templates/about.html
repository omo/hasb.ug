What is hasb.ug?

Hasb.ug is a URL shortener for bug tracker URLs.

Hasb.ug serves short domains like wkb.ug and redirects short URLs such as http://wkb.ug/63606 to long ones - For wkb.ug63606/, that is https://bugs.webkit.org/show_bug.cgi?id=63606.

You can tell your own domain or host name to hasb.ug service and bind a bug-tracker URL to the name. Hasb.ug then expands given short URL and redirects it to the long one you told.

This is called a shortener. Sign in using your Github account to let hasb.ug know about your shorteners.

Hasb.ug also provides names under hasb.ug domain for you. For example, you can create less.hasb.ug as a shortener for https://github.com/cloudhead/less.js/issues/. You don't need to pay for any domain names if you use names under hasb.ug domain.

Domain Setup

Once purchased a domain for your bug, you need to

  • Point 50.19.127.155 as your domain's A record. This is the IP address of hasb.ug server. Then,
  • Tell the domain to hasb.ug through the console page.

Note: For hasb.ug sub-domains, what you need to do is just to tell it from the cosole. No DNS configuration is needed.
